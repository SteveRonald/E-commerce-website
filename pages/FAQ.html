Frequently Asked Questions
  • What is EcoNest?
    EcoNest is an online store dedicated to providing eco-friendly, sustainable products for your home and lifestyle. We believe in making it easy for everyone to live greener!
  • How do I place an order?
    Simply browse our shop, add your favorite products to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You’ll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and secure online payments. All transactions are encrypted for your safety.
  • How long does delivery take?
    Orders within Kenya are delivered within 2-4 business days. You’ll receive a tracking link once your order is shipped.
  • Can I return or exchange a product?
    Yes! If you’re not satisfied, you can return or exchange your product within 7 days of delivery. Please see our returns policy for details.
  • Are your products really eco-friendly?
    Absolutely. All our products are carefully selected for their sustainability, biodegradability, or upcycled nature. We work with trusted suppliers who share our green values.
  • How can I contact EcoNest?
    You can reach us via our contact page or email us at support@econest.co.ke.
